How the scoring works
Formula: weighted pips + diversity + number spread + port bonus - robber risk + expansion.

Weighted pips: ore and wheat are valued slightly higher, brick and wood get an early-game boost, and sheep is slightly discounted.

Diversity: three unique resources score better than repeating the same production profile.

Number spread: different token numbers reduce correlation and smooth income over time.

Ports: matching 2:1 ports matter most, generic 3:1 ports still help, and off-resource ports get a smaller bonus.

Robber risk: overly concentrated production gets penalized because one blocked tile can cripple the spot.

Expansion: nearby follow-up intersections add value when road options remain strong.
